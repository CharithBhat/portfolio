Okay, what is this about?



So chances are if you're here, you've probably been invited by an existing member of the Hivenet.


In short, Hivenet is a social network that's built only for students and alums of Kssem and Kssa. Oh and it's also free.


we recent graduates of Kssem built it so that all the generations of both our colleges can connect with each other.

What can I do on the app?





You can use the HiveNet to:


⚪ Look up people at your college (search people by name, branch & semester).


⚪ Engage with your college's feed.


⚪ Find like-minded people (You can add stuff about yourself on your profile).

How do I download the app?


The app is an Apk file. You can download it from the google drive link given below. Your phone will likely ask you to specifically allow this Apk file because by default Apk's are not trusted by your phone.


Note: after downloading and installing the hivenet. Send your google account email to your friend so that they can invite your email on the app. without having your email invited you will not be able to sign up.


ps: currently IOS is not supported.



How do I invite a friend?



First send this website to your friend, so they can download the app.


After that go to the invite screen in your app and add your friend's google account email ID, That's it!
